Complete Denture and Partial Denture: Restoring Your Smile

What are Dentures?

Complete Dentures

Complete dentures are removable dental prosthetics designed to replace all missing teeth in the upper or lower jaw. These prostheses are crafted to resemble natural teeth and gums, providing full restoration of the smile and oral functionality for individuals who have lost all their natural teeth.

Partial Dentures

Partial dentures are removable dental appliances used to replace one or more missing teeth when some natural teeth remain. They consist of artificial teeth attached to a framework that clasps onto existing teeth for support. Partial dentures not only restore aesthetics but also enhance the functionality of the remaining natural teeth.

Benefits and Uses

Restoration

Complete and partial dentures are commonly used to restore a complete and functional smile for individuals who have experienced tooth loss. These dental prosthetics not only improve aesthetics but also play a crucial role in supporting facial structure and preventing potential oral health issues associated with missing teeth.

Functionality

These dental prosthetics help in restoring the ability to chew, speak, and smile confidently. Complete dentures provide a comprehensive solution for those with total tooth loss, while partial dentures offer targeted restoration, maintaining the functionality of the remaining natural teeth while filling gaps caused by missing ones.

The Process

Evaluation

The process begins with a thorough evaluation of the patient's oral health, including an assessment of the condition of existing teeth, gum health, and overall oral structures. This evaluation guides the dentist in determining the most suitable type of denture for the individual's needs.

Impressions

Once the evaluation is complete, the next step involves taking impressions of the patient's mouth. These impressions serve as the basis for crafting custom-fitted dentures that will provide optimal comfort, functionality, and aesthetics. Precision is key to ensure a secure and natural fit.

Fitting

After crafting the dentures, the fitting process ensures that the prosthetics align seamlessly with the patient's natural oral structures. Dentists make necessary adjustments to achieve a comfortable and secure fit, allowing individuals to regain confidence in their smile and enjoy improved oral function.
